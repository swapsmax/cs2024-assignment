Introducing Our World-Class Skin Treatments

In recent years, skin rejuvenation treatments have evolved and developed by leaps and bounds. Thanks to sophisticated technologies and breakthrough processes, reversing the years on your skin is no longer a painful or time-consuming affair.

  • The Carbon Laser Peel firms up the skin by applying carbon powder to it, which is subsequently removed using a laser. Because the carbon powder can enter deep into the pores, it allows the laser to more efficiently focus its energy and target active acne.


    This carbon powder can absorb dirt from deep within the pores. It can also assist in reducing acne irritation and exfoliate the skin. Because the carbon particles are so tiny, they can enter pores and hard-to-reach locations, allowing the laser to efficiently distribute energy to these areas.


    When the carbon powder is broken down, the little amount of heat created promotes collagen formation in the skin, resulting in plumper, radiant skin with less fine lines and wrinkles. At the same time, the heat kills the P-acne bacteria that causes pimples, resulting in better acne control.


    The Carbon Laser Peel is a rapid and painless procedure with minimal downtime. During treatment, it does not disrupt the continuity of skin tissue.

  • A chemical peel is a mixture of glycolic, salicylic, and lactic acids with a pH of roughly 1.9. It is advised for the treatment of fine wrinkles, skin whitening, oily skin, and acne. It is mostly utilized in our clinic for oil management, acne treatment, and dull skin.


    The Chemical Peel will benefit patients with:


    Clogged pores and blackheads: To avoid irritation and acne, a Chemical Peel removes blocked pores and blackheads.


    Pimples and acne: The acids in the Chemical Peel dry up existing inflammatory acne while also killing the P.acne bacterium that causes acne.


    Oily skin: A Chemical Peel can help patients with oily skin who want to limit their oil production while also drying up extra sebum.


    Chemical Peels can be applied every 1-2 weeks until desired results are achieved. While the recommended wait time between procedures is two weeks, those with severe acne can undergo this treatment every 7 – 10 days.
