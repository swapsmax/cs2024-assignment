Face, The Most Important Part Of Your Body.

It's not always about looks. It's also about having a good skin health experience. You might be shocked to learn that face treatments to renew the skin can be dated back to the 1500s and 1600s. The quest for bright and regenerated skin has inspired a plethora of novel cosmetic treatments in the years thereafter. Every now and again, our skin needs some attention. Our outstanding face treatments should leave you feeling energetic and tranquil, while also revealing smoother, softer, more moisturized, and radiant skin.

  • Our facial lifting procedures help reduce sagging skin to give you a youthful appearance, while being designed to be safe and minimally-invasive.


    Thread lifting is a minimally-invasive anti-aging surgery that includes inserting absorbable threads into various parts of the face, chin, or neck using a cannula or needle. Depending on the type and amount of threads used, this helps restore our facial features by raising drooping tissues and rearranging facial volumes to obtain a more youthful appearance.


    Some effects such as lifting or contouring may be visible immediately after the procedure. However, full effects of the thread lift will usually take 3 months to show as the new collagen forms around the thread.


    Downtime is fairly minimal. Mild bruising may occur which is usually easily covered up by cosmetics, and typically lasts 4-7 days.

  • Ulthera, also known as Ultherapy, is a non-surgical method approved by the FDA that uses high-intensity focused ultrasound radiation to generate tiny and precise thermal coagulation zones (TCZ) at certain depths under the skin.


    This method allows the surgeon to precisely target different layers of the face in order to tighten and raise drooping facial tissues while protecting the underlying skin.


    Ultherapy can be safely performed to treat the following areas:

    Forehead | Around Eyes | Lower Face

    Double Chin | Neck | Cheeks
